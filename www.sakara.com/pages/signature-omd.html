Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a variety of different meal programs designed to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle. You can choose between our signature organic meal delivery programs with the option to do a one-week trial program or a recurring subscription program.You can choose the number of days you would like to receive meals (3 or 5 days), and depending on your delivery zone, you may also choose which meals you would like to receive (breakfast, lunch and/or dinner).

We also offer our Level II Detox program once a month which is our intensive cleanse designed to give you a total body reset, as well as a 20-Day Bridal Program which is designed to help you feel like your best self on your big day.

A Sakara subscription is the easiest, most convenient, and most cost-effective option for living the Sakara Life! Because we know that the most life-changing results occur when you eat our way the majority of the time, a Sakara subscription ensures you never have to miss a single week of clean, wholesome meals - they will automatically renew each week without having to place an order!

Subscription pricing is already discounted plus we waive the delivery fee for you. While you cannot cancel your first week, there is absolutely no commitment beyond that, and you can manage your subscription calendar, including skipping weeks, to best fit your schedule. All changes must be made by Wednesday at midnight EST for your program the following week. We’ll charge your credit card on Thursday mornings for the upcoming week’s program unless you are paused or canceled. In addition, subscribers benefit from increased program flexibility as well as exclusive access to products, special programs, and events.

We offer a set menu of delicious meals that rotates weekly, as well as seasonally.

Our Signature Nutrition Program includes up to 3 meals per day (breakfast, lunch, and/or dinner, depending on choices available in your location) + Detox Tea + one of our functional wellness supplements. Most programs come with our Complete Probiotic because it is specially formulated to maintain a diverse and balanced gut microbiome—the secret to bloat-free digestion, healthy weight loss, hormone balance, and glowing skin. The Detox Tea included is a unique, caffeine-free blend of cleansing plants and herbs that gently detoxifies the body and helps increase your daily water intake. Additionally, you receive access to our team of Sakara Wellness Coaches who are available to help support you on your wellness journey. Contact info@sakaralife.com to learn more.
